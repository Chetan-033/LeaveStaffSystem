it's my academic year-2024-25 project
The Staff Leave System is designed for college staff to easily apply for leave. 
The system enables the Head of Department (HOD) and the Principal to manage leave requests efficiently.
Staff Leave Application: Staff members can apply for leave through a user-friendly interface.
Leave Management: The HOD and Principal can view, accept, or reject leave requests.
